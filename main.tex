\textbf{Course Summary}: An introduction to Mathematical Analysis.  We will cover the topics including the axiom of completeness, bounds, sequences, series, introductory topology of $\mathbb{R}^n$, introductory set theory, continuity, and derivatives, with mathematical rigor.  The textbook for reference will be Stephen Abbott's \textit{Understanding Analysis}.
